Calories & Macros

Nutrition does not have to be very fancy. Start by looking at how much you eat in a day, then try to balance protein, carbohydrates, and fats.

Macronutrients

  • Protein: repairs and builds muscle.
  • Carbohydrates: give you energy for workouts.
  • Fats: support hormones and long-term energy.

Simple Meal Steps

  1. Pick a protein (chicken, tofu, beans, eggs).
  2. Add a carb (rice, potatoes, pasta, whole grains).
  3. Include a healthy fat (olive oil, avocado, nuts).
  4. Add vegetables or fruit for vitamins and fiber.
