Why Sleep Matters

Muscles grow and recover while you sleep, not just during your workout. Poor sleep can lead to low energy and slower progress.

Healthy Sleep Habits

  • Aim for 7–9 hours of sleep most nights.
  • Try to go to bed and wake up at the same time.
  • Limit screens 30–60 minutes before bed.
  • Keep your room cool, dark, and quiet.

Simple Sleep Tracking Ideas

You can write down a few things each day to see patterns:

  1. What time you went to bed.
  2. What time you woke up.
  3. How rested you felt (1–10).
  4. Any caffeine or big meals late at night.
